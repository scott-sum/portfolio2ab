The Price is Now Right

Built with: Python Flask, Bootstrap, SQLite

Deployed on Heroku at price-is-now-right.herokuapp.com


We all have things we want to buy but at times, we may not be able to afford them.

In addition, with the advent of online shopping, many stores often release flash sales that many people miss because they are not constantly keeping track of the site.

The item we want may go on sale without us knowing!

The Price is Now Right is a Python Flask app which allows you to keep track of the price of an item on Amazon.

The user can specify their budget and the app will keep checking the price for them. Once the price falls below their budget, they will recieve an email notification.

This app allows for signup and login functionality and routing using GET and POST methods. It stores the users information in an SQlite database

It uses many libraries such as Flask-login, flask-wtf, flask-sqlalchemy, smptlib, requests.
